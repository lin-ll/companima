Bringing companionship to seniors and providing homes for animals.

 
 
 

Mission

In a nation with over fifty million seniors and more than six million shelter animals, there is an opportunity to bring these communities together in a way that decreases health issues for seniors and alleviates the overpopulation strain on animal shelters. Companima forges connections between seniors and abandoned pets to extend and enhance the well-being and productivity of our nation's aging population while helping address our biggest economic and healthcare challenges. 

 

Goals

Our theory of change is simple: we can have a tremendously positive effect on the well-being (both mental and physical) of American seniors. By doing so, we will help create a healthier senior population while also reducing healthcare costs. Moreover, by connecting seniors with pets from animal shelters, we will be reducing the burdens currently shouldered by overcrowded animal shelters.
